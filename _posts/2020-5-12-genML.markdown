We held our final review for a new course, [Generative Machine Learning for Architecture](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1o5_gQN5ByytWSoe67LLAIaFzEG86ReY_MXBjZDpCF_w/edit?usp=sharing) (co-instructor [Ben Aranda](http://arandalasch.com/work/featured/)). This was a new experiment at Cooper where we embedded a small group of engineering students into the architecture studio. Although the semester was quite disrupted by COVID-19, we are still really impressed with the output of this course.

For example, Juan Cardona did some striking images imagining new textures in a new landscape:
